In 2023, TikTok would throw your stream into the For You Feed (FYF) just to see what happened. In 2026, the algorithm is far more conservative. It tests your stream with a tiny "seed audience" first. If they leave immediately, the test is over.

## Why Does the Algorithm Feel "Broken"? (3 Real Reasons)

### 1. The "Hook" Mismatch
Most small streamers start their broadcast with: "I'll wait for more people to join before starting." On TikTok, this is suicide. Because TikTok is a vertical scroll platform, you are competing with every video in the world. If you aren't doing something interesting the second they swipe to you, they are gone.

### 2. The Saturation Effect
There are 10x more TikTok creators now than 12 months ago. The supply of "small gaming streamers" has exceeded the demand. If your stream looks exactly like 1,000 other people playing COD or Roblox, the algorithm has no reason to favor you.

### 3. The Interaction Wall
TikTok is heavily promoting **Live Gifts** and **Interactive Tools** (like TikFinity). Streams using these tools generate more algorithm signals. If you are just a static webcam and gameplay, you are fighting with one hand tied behind your back.

## FAQ: Hard Truths for Small Streamers

**"If I have 0 viewers, am I shadowbanned?"**
Probably not. Most "shadowbans" are just low retention scores from previous streams. TikTok's algorithm remembers if your last 5 streams failed to hold an audience.

**"Is TikTok Live Studio really better than OBS?"**
Stability-wise, TikTok favors its own app, but if your content is good enough, OBS (via Virtual Camera or Stream Key) is fine. Tool choice matters far less than content hooks.

**"Should I use 'Promote' to break the 0-viewer streak?"**
Not recommended. Paid promotion often brings "low quality" viewers who swipe away quickly, which can actually hurt your organic retention score in the long run.

## Conclusion

TikTok Live isn't broken; there's just no more "free lunch." The platform has moved from the "experiment phase" to the "retention phase." If you want the reach you had a year ago, you have to work twice as hard in the **first 10 seconds**. Stop waiting for the algorithm to fix itself and start giving it the data it needs (retention and interaction) to justify pushing your stream to more people.
